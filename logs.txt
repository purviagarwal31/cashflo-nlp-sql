User Query: What is total revenue?
SQL: SELECT SUM(grand_total) FROM invoices WHERE status = 'paid';
Result:    SUM(grand_total)
0       84969063.07
Explanation: This SQL query is finding the total amount of money made from invoices that have been paid. 

Here's how it works:
1. It looks at a list of invoices.
2. It only considers the invoices that have been paid (status = 'paid').
3. It adds up the total amount of money from all those paid invoices (SUM of grand_total).
4. The result is the total revenue.

Explanation: This SQL query is doing the following:

User Query: Top vendors by revenue
SQL: SELECT vendors.name, SUM(invoices.grand_total) AS revenue FROM invoices JOIN vendors ON invoices.vendor_id = vendors.id WHERE invoices.status = 'paid' GROUP BY vendors.name ORDER BY revenue DESC;
Result:                            name      revenue
0         AlphaConsult Partners  18309145.92
1        DataPipe Analytics LLP  13671902.06
2          PrimePack Industries  12021668.78
3             OmniTech Hardware   8806471.60
4             NovaChem Supplies   7381760.17
5       RapidBuild Construction   5736811.09
6   SafeGuard Security Services   4469443.44
7   TechServe Solutions Pvt Ltd   3235111.75
8           PrintMax Stationery   2637799.00
9      GreenLogistics Transport   2606373.07
10               CloudNet Infra   2582262.12
11            MegaTrade Exports   2356822.31
12       BrightStar IT Services   1153491.76
Explanation: This SQL query is doing the following:

1. It's looking at two sets of data: "invoices" (bills that have been sent) and "vendors" (the people or companies that sent the bills).
2. It's matching the invoices with the vendors who sent them.
3. It's only looking at invoices that have been paid.
4. It's adding up the total amount of money each vendor has been paid (this is called the "revenue").
5. It's grouping the results by vendor name, so it can show how much each vendor has been paid.
6. Finally, it's sorting the results so that the vendors who have been paid the most are at the top of the list.

So, the query is answering the question: "Which vendors have earned the most money from paid invoices?"
